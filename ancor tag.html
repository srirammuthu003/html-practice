Toyoto Innova

The Toyota Innova Crysta is a premium, spacious MPV known for its comfort, reliability, and powerful diesel engine.

Toyota

🛠️ Engine & Performance

Powered by a 2.4 L diesel engine delivering 148 bhp and 343 Nm torque, mated to a 5-speed manual gearbox; rear-wheel drive ensures robustness for Indian roads 

A soft suspension setup makes city and highway travel smooth, though it can feel a bit less stable above ~100 km/h
